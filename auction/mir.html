hilies, also spelled as chillies, are spicy fruits from plants belonging to the Capsicum genus. These fruits are widely used in culinary applications around the world, adding heat and flavor to various dishes.The heat of chilies is often measured on the Scoville Heat Scale. This scale quantifies the amount of capsaicin, the compound responsible for the heat in chilies.Chilies are a key ingredient in many cuisines around the world, providing both heat and flavor to dishes.They are used fresh, dried, or ground into powders to season sauces, stews, curries, soups, and marinades.Hot sauces and chili pastes are common condiments made from chilies.

Why To use the Palak?

Flavor Enhancement:
Chilies add a distinct and complex flavor to dishes. The heat from chilies stimulates taste buds, enhancing the overall flavor profile of a dish.
Versatility:
Chilies come in various shapes, sizes, and heat levels. This diversity allows for their use in a wide range of cuisines and dishes, from mild and sweet to extremely spicy.
Cultural Significance:
Chilies are integral to the culinary traditions of many cultures. They are key ingredients in the cuisines of regions such as Mexico, Thailand, India, China, and many others, contributing to the unique identity of those cuisines.
